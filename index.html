üé≠ Whisper Commands

/whisper send
Send an anonymous whisper to a specific user. They can accept or decline your message.
/whisper random
Send a whisper to a random server member. Great for meeting new people!
/whisper list
View all your active anonymous chats and their status.
/whisper pending
See whispers you've sent that are waiting for a response.
/whisper received
View whispers you've received that you haven't responded to yet.
/whisper end
Vote to end an active anonymous chat. Both parties must agree.
